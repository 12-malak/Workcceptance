Pros of Diversity

Diversity can provide many potential advantages to organizations. On one level it helps organizations to reach out to diverse customer groups and markets, and on another level by allowing for a variety of perspectives, it promotes innovativeness and superior work outcomes and performance.

What is Workcceptance

Workcceptance is the discourse increasingly shifting to one of inclusion, over and beyond that of diversity. The founders of Workcceptance launched the Initiative to bring together tech and diverse leaders with potential to develop innovative digital solutions and address challenges faced by the workplace. Innovative tech and entrepreneurial solutions are needed to tap into the power of digital transformation in order to rebound and pivot to new opportunities in the long term, through:

  • ❒ more diversified and resilient business models
  • ❒ improved platforms for demographics of interest in the diversity
  • ❒ better networking and exchanges, and
  • ❒ a more sustainable practices of inclusion in workplace.

How to join Workcceptance Ideathon

1. Sign up for Ideathon

Sign up for the Ideathon to solve challenges in the workplace. Start developing your initial idea that matches our challenges. If you don’t have a team, you can team up with other participants during the Ideathon Online Event.



2. Participate in Ideathon Online Event and Team Up

During this event, learn about brainstorming ideas & design thinking, team up with other participants, develop a project idea together and get feedback from experts in the field. Submit your final idea before the deadline. Do not forget to include a presentation, idea brief and optionally a video. The access details to the online event are shared a few days before the event by email.



Learn about Challenges

3. Join Online Pitch Event as Finalist and Win Fantastic Prizes

After the submission of ideas, the Workcceptance selection team chooses the finalist teams. Finalists are invited to pitch their ideas to businesses, and investors at a dedicated event. Winners are announced during the Pitch Event, receiving cash and in-kind prizes, technical support, mentoring and investment by our valuable partners.

What do we have at Workcceptance Ideathon for You?

Relevance

  • Your idea addresses one or more of the challenges listed on Workcceptance website.

Business Potential

  • The idea has market potential with a sustainable revenue model. It solves a particular problem for a target group of audience.

Solution Design

  • The solution has a modular and flexible design that meets the business requirements. You should provide an implementation plan including feature set and recommended techstack.

Technical Implementation

  • The idea is feasible to implement using existing well-adopted technologies. You are able to estimate resources (staff, infrastructure, funding) required to develop a minimum viable product.

Scalability

  • It is possible to expand the solution to and/or replicate it in other countries.

Collaboration

  • The team is made of members from the tech community and workplace. The solution has the capacity to engage the community in the development for example open source approach.

Inclusiveness

  • The idea is inclusive in regards to accessibility for disable people, remote worker and immigrants.
